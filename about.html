What am I doing right now?

Pursuing a Master's Degree in Interaction Design from the Industrial Design Centre (IDC) at IIT Bombay.

What have I done before?

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Fashion Communication from NIFT (National Institute of Fashion Technology), Hyderabad - I studied Graphic Design, Writing, Photography, Styling, amongst other things.

What industry experience do I have?

During my bachelor's, I interned at Beard Design (UI/UX Team) & Ogilvy One (Creative & Planning Team). I have also done a bunch of freelance projects through college.

Why did I steer towards Interaction Design from Communication Design?

An enthusiasm for solving problems and an appreciation for the the user-centric approach that Interaction Design holds at its core brought me to this field.
